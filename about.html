How I see the world

My View on Worldwide Theories

01
The Big Bang Theory

The universe began approximately 13.8 billion years ago from an infinitely dense singularity. Everything we see — every star, planet, and galaxy — expanded from that single point. What fascinates me is that a Belgian priest, Georges Lemaître, was the one who first proposed it — proof that science and faith are not always opposites.

02
Darwin's Theory of Evolution

All life on earth shares a common ancestor and has evolved through natural selection over billions of years. The species that survive are not the strongest or the smartest — they are the ones most adaptable to change. I think about this beyond biology — it applies to people, businesses, and ideas too. Adapt or become irrelevant.
